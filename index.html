About Drop for Africa

Drop for Africa is an experience-first safari agency built in Kilifi, Kenya. We combine expert guides, luxury transfers, and bold storytelling to make every itinerary feel like a story worth sharing.

Travel with us to landmarks such as Maasai Mara, Amboseli, Tsavo East, Tsavo West, Naivasha, Watamu, Serengeti and the iconic Ngorongoro Crater.
